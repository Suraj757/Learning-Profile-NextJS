Learning Style Cards
Instant access to each student's learning preferences, successful strategies, and intervention suggestions.
Pro Tips for Using Learning Style Cards
1
Keep them visible: Place cards on your desk or clipboard for quick reference during lessons.
2
Use during planning: Review cards when creating lesson plans to ensure you're including strategies for all learning styles.
3
Share with substitutes: Leave cards with substitute teachers so they can maintain your differentiated approach.
4
Update regularly: Add notes about what works and update as you learn more about each student.
